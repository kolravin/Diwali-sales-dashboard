Project Overview

This Diwali Sales Dashboard was built using Microsoft Power BI to analyze festive season sales performance across customers, product categories, gender, age groups, occupations, and geography (states + zones).
The goal of this dashboard is to help businesses understand who is buying, what they are buying, and where sales are strongest, so that marketing and inventory decisions can be optimized for future campaigns.

Detailed Analysis & Insights
1. Sales by Gender

Female customers contribute ~74M, while Male customers contribute ~32M
 Insight: Female customers are the primary revenue driver, contributing the majority of festive sales.

 Action:
Focus marketing campaigns (offers, bundles, influencer targeting) more toward the female buyer segment.

 Business Decision:
Increase inventory and promotions in categories highly preferred by female shoppers during Diwali.
